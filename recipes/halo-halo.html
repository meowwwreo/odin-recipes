Halo-Halo Especial

Description

This refreshing snack of sweet fruit, beans, and leche flan is topped off with creamy shaved ice and ice cream.

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 oz) can jackfruit, drained and diced
  • 1 (12 oz) jar sweetened white kidney beans
  • 1 (12 oz) jar red beans, drained
  • shaved ice
  • 1/2 cup evaporated milk, or to taste
  • 1/2 cup vanilla ice cream, or to taste

Steps

  1. Place jack fruit in a bowl. Add white beans and red beans and mix thoroughly.
  2. Place 2 tablespoons of the jackfruit mixture in a tall glass. Fill glass 2/3-full of shaved ice. Pour evaporated milk over the shaved ice. Mix using a teaspoon. Top with 2 tablespoons vanilla ice cream.
  3. Repeat layering the halo halo in 3 more glasses.
